Abstract

Avia is an AI-driven assistant designed to simplify the flight training process for students and new pilots. By integrating voice-based interactions and LLMs, Avia provides real-time in-flight assistance, personalized performance analysis, and streamlined learning tools. The system includes features such as emergency response guidance, pre-flight route planning, and post-flight performance summaries. Utilizing data from digital logbooks and in-flight devices, Avia creates a tailored learning experience to improve safety, efficiency, and confidence.

Target Customers

  • Student Pilots
  • Pilots in Training
